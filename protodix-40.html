PROTODIX-40

( PANTOPRAZOLE 40MG GASTRO RESISTANT TABLET )

A trusted medication (Pantoprazole 40mg) designed to provide effective relief from acidity, heartburn, and acid reflux by significantly reducing stomach acid production.

Mechanism of Action

PROTODIX-40 contains Pantoprazole, a proton pump inhibitor. It works by irreversibly binding to and inhibiting the hydrogen-potassium ATPase pump (the "proton pump") located in the stomach's parietal cells. This action effectively blocks the final step in acid secretion, leading to a significant and long-lasting reduction in stomach acid levels for up to 24 hours.

Common Uses & Indications

  • Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD): For symptomatic relief and healing of acid-related damage.
  • Peptic Ulcer Disease: Treats and helps prevent both stomach and duodenal ulcers.
  • Zollinger-Ellison Syndrome: Manages conditions involving excessive stomach acid production.
  • Erosive Esophagitis: Heals and maintains the esophagus from acid erosion.
  • NSAID-Associated Ulcer Prevention: Protects the stomach lining for patients on long-term NSAID therapy.

Dosage, Side Effects & Considerations

Typical Dosing:

  • The standard dose is one 40mg tablet taken once daily.
  • For best results, take on an empty stomach, approximately one hour before a meal.
  • Available in oral tablet and intravenous (IV) formulations for clinical use.

Common Side Effects:

  • Headache, diarrhea or constipation, nausea, abdominal pain, and dizziness.

Important Considerations:

  • Long-term use may increase the risk of bone fractures and can lead to Vitamin B12 or magnesium deficiencies.
  • May interact with certain medications, including warfarin and clopidogrel. Always consult a healthcare provider.
