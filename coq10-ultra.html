CO Q 10 ULTRA

( CO-ENZYME Q 10 300MG+L-ARGININE 250MG+ OMEGA-3 FATTY ACID 250MG+ SELENIUM DIOXIDE MONOHYDRATED EQ. TO ELEMENTAL SELENIUM 70MCG+ ZINC OXIDE EQ. TO ELEMENTAL ZINC 20MG SOFT GELATIN CAPSULE )

This formulation is a cardiovascular and antioxidant supplement particularly beneficial for patients with cardiovascular risk factors, heart disease, or those seeking comprehensive antioxidant support.

Individual Components & Mode of Action

  • Coenzyme Q10 (300mg) - Antioxidant enzyme cofactor
    • Supports mitochondrial energy production (ATP synthesis)
    • Cardioprotective, improves heart muscle function
    • Reduces oxidative stress
  • L-Arginine (250mg) - Semi-essential amino acid
    • Precursor to nitric oxide (NO) production
    • Promotes vasodilation and improved blood flow
    • Supports endothelial function
  • Omega-3 Fatty Acids (250mg) - Essential fatty acids (EPA/DHA)
    • Anti-inflammatory effects
    • Supports cardiovascular health, brain function
    • Reduces triglycerides and improves lipid profile
  • Selenium Dioxide (70mcg) - Essential trace mineral
    • Antioxidant enzyme cofactor (glutathione peroxidase)
    • Supports immune function and thyroid health
    • Protects against oxidative damage
  • Zinc Oxide (2mg) - Essential mineral
    • Immune system support
    • Wound healing, protein synthesis
    • Antioxidant properties

Primary Uses & Indications

  • Cardiology: Congestive heart failure support, Post-myocardial infarction recovery, Hypertension management, Atherosclerosis prevention, Statin-induced CoQ10 depletion.
  • Endocrinology: Diabetic cardiovascular complications, Metabolic syndrome, Insulin resistance support.
  • Nephrology: Chronic kidney disease with cardiovascular risk, Diabetic nephropathy.
  • Geriatrics: Age-related cardiovascular decline, Cognitive function support, General antioxidant support.
  • Sports Medicine: Exercise performance enhancement, Post-exercise recovery, Cardiovascular fitness support.
  • Reproductive Health: Male fertility support (L-arginine for erectile function), Sperm quality improvement.

Mechanism of Action & Clinical Benefits

Mechanism of Action:

  • Cellular Energy: CoQ10 enhances mitochondrial ATP production.
  • Vascular Health: L-arginine promotes NO-mediated vasodilation.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Omega-3s reduce inflammatory markers.
  • Antioxidant Protection: Selenium and zinc support antioxidant enzyme systems.
  • Synergistic Effect: Combined ingredients provide comprehensive cardiovascular support.

Clinical Benefits:

  • Improved heart muscle contractility & endothelial function.
  • Reduced oxidative stress & better exercise tolerance.
  • Improved lipid profile & enhanced immune function.

Dosage, Precautions & Side Effects

Dosage:

  • Typical: 1 tablet daily with meals.
  • Timing: Preferably with breakfast or lunch (fat-soluble components).
  • Duration: Long-term supplementation as advised by healthcare provider.

Contraindications & Precautions:

  • Caution with blood thinners (Omega-3 interactions).
  • Monitor in patients with low blood pressure (L-arginine).
  • Avoid in active bleeding disorders.
  • Consult healthcare provider if pregnant/breastfeeding.

Side Effects:

  • Generally well-tolerated, but may include mild GI upset, a fishy aftertaste (Omega-3), and rare allergic reactions.
