PRECOXIB-75

PREGABALIN 75MG+ ETORICOXIB 60MG PROLONGED RELEASE TABLET

A dual-action medication combining Pregabalin and Etoricoxib to provide broad-spectrum relief from complex pain syndromes involving both neuropathic and inflammatory components.

Individual Components & Actions

  • Pregabalin (75mg): An anticonvulsant that targets neuropathic (nerve) pain by reducing abnormal electrical activity in the brain and interfering with pain signals from damaged nerves.
  • Etoricoxib (60mg): A selective COX-2 inhibitor (NSAID) that provides powerful anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving effects by blocking chemical messengers responsible for inflammation and swelling.

Primary Uses & Mechanism

Prescribed For:

  • Chronic pain conditions with both inflammatory and neuropathic elements.
  • Pain associated with Osteoarthritis or Rheumatoid Arthritis.
  • Complex pain syndromes where single-agent therapy is insufficient.

How They Work Together:

  • Pregabalin specifically targets and calms nerve-related pain.
  • Etoricoxib reduces inflammation and the general pain it causes.
  • This synergistic combination provides broader and more effective pain relief than either medication used alone.

Considerations & Side Effects

Important Considerations:

  • Pregabalin can cause drowsiness and dizziness; use caution when operating machinery.
  • Etoricoxib may carry cardiovascular risks; use with caution in patients with heart conditions.
  • Both medications can interact with other drugs. Inform your doctor of all medications you are taking.

Common Side Effects:

  • Dizziness and drowsiness
  • Swelling (edema) of hands or feet
  • Headache or nausea
  • Potential for increased blood pressure
