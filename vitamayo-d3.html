VITAMAYO-D3

( methylcobalamin 1500mcg+alpha lipoic acid 100mg+benfotiamine 15mg+folic acid 1.5mg+chromium polynicotinate 200mcg+Inositol 100mg+pyridoxine HCl 3.5mg+Vitamin D3 1000 IU +calcium carbonate 500mg tablet )

A comprehensive multivitamin and multimineral supplement, particularly popular for managing diabetic complications, supporting overall metabolic health, and ensuring robust nerve and bone function.

Individual Components & Actions

  • Methylcobalamin (1500mcg): Supports nerve function and red blood cell formation.
  • Alpha Lipoic Acid (100mg): A powerful antioxidant that protects nerve health.
  • Benfotiamine (15mg): A superior form of Vitamin B1 for nerve function and glucose metabolism.
  • Folic Acid (1.5mg): Essential for DNA synthesis and cell growth.
  • Chromium & Inositol: Support glucose metabolism and insulin sensitivity.
  • Pyridoxine HCl (Vitamin B6): Crucial for protein metabolism and neurotransmitter synthesis.
  • Vitamin D3 (1000 IU) & Calcium (500mg): A vital duo for bone health, immune function, and calcium absorption.

Primary Uses & Key Benefits

Primary Uses:

  • Diabetic neuropathy and nerve pain management
  • Correction of multiple nutritional deficiencies
  • Metabolic and nutritional support for diabetic patients
  • Bone health and peripheral neuropathy support

Key Benefits:

  • Offers comprehensive nerve support and antioxidant protection.
  • Aids in metabolic support, particularly for diabetes.
  • Ensures robust bone health maintenance.

Dosage & Important Notes

Typical Dosage:

  • One tablet daily, or as directed by a healthcare provider.
  • Best taken with or after meals to improve absorption of all components.
  • Should be taken consistently at the same time each day for best results.

Important Notes:

  • Generally well-tolerated, but may cause mild GI upset in some individuals.
  • Always consult your healthcare provider before starting, especially if you have existing medical conditions or are taking other medications.
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
